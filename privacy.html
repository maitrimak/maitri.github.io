Privacy Statement

1. Information Collection, Use, and Disclosure

We are collecting personal information when you are registering online.

2. Protection of Your Personal Information

Your personal information is being protected by security safeguards appropriate to the sensitivity of the information. We are not sharing your information with any third party outside of our organization unless required by law. When we are sharing your information, we are limiting disclosure according to the identified purposes.

3. Contact Information

If you are supplying us with your contact information, you may be hearing from us about important information related to your interest in volunteering. If you do not wish to receive updates about other Volunteer Dufferin programs, services, or opportunities, please email us.

4. Links on Our Website

Because we are trying to make your website experience informational and resourceful, we are also providing a number of links to third-party sites. Volunteer Dufferin is assuming no responsibility for the information practices of external sites. We are encouraging you to review each site's privacy policy before disclosing any personally identifiable information.

5. Your Access to and Control Over Information

We are striving to ensure that your personal information is accurate, complete, and up-to-date for the purposes it is being used. You are having the right to access, update, and correct your personal information by contacting us at the provided email address.
